# Energy, Utilities & Power Systems - Real-Time Intelligence Use Cases

## Industry Overview

The energy, utilities, and power systems industry is undergoing a significant transformation driven by the transition to renewable energy, grid modernization, increasing demand from data centers and electric vehicles, and the need for operational efficiency. Real-time intelligence plays a critical role in enabling utilities to optimize operations, predict failures, balance supply and demand, and deliver reliable service to customers.

Microsoft Fabric Real-Time Intelligence provides an integrated platform for ingesting, processing, analyzing, and acting on streaming data from smart meters, grid sensors, renewable energy systems, and operational technology (OT) systems.

## Implementation Effort Rating Criteria

| Rating | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Low** | Primarily software/analytics solution leveraging existing data infrastructure. Minimal new hardware required. Can be implemented in 3-6 months. |
| **Medium** | Requires moderate integration work with existing systems, some new sensor deployment, or ML model development. Typical implementation timeline of 6-12 months. |
| **High** | Significant infrastructure changes required, including extensive sensor deployment, legacy system integration, or complex edge computing. Implementation timeline of 12-24 months. |
| **Very High** | Major transformation involving multiple systems, significant hardware deployment, organizational change management, and regulatory considerations. Timeline exceeds 24 months. |

## Value of Implementation Rating Criteria

| Rating | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Low** | Incremental operational improvements with modest ROI. Nice-to-have capability. |
| **Medium** | Clear business case with measurable benefits. Competitive advantage in certain scenarios. |
| **High** | Strong ROI with significant cost savings or revenue impact. Addresses important business or regulatory requirements. |
| **Very High** | Transformational impact on operations, safety, or customer experience. Critical for business continuity or regulatory compliance. Industry-leading capability. |

## Key Technology Components (Microsoft Fabric Real-Time Intelligence)
